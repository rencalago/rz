Lalimar Beach Resort - Infinite Relaxation

Most people in Negros Oriental knows the best place to visit in the north. Without a doubt, Lalimar Beach Resort would be the first on the list.


The resort is perfect for holiday and weekend bonding place. Lalimar has several rooms to accommodate guests who wants to stay for the night. Cottages are also available for day-use guests.


Its infinity pool is undeniably the main highlight of its amenities fronting the tanon strait. Perhaps the best spot to see the sunrise in the morning.

You don't go hungry here. There is wide food selection at their restaurant. Our personal favorites are shrimp gambas, fried chicken, carbonarra and margaritta pizza.


Lalimar is also the favorite venue for weddings and birthdays. A place where you can have hassle-free event with functions that can handle hundreds of guests.
