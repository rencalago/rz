Travel to Baguio City: Celebration and Exploration

Baguio City is one of our favorite cities. That is why we opted to celebrate Enzo's 3rd birthday here. We spent a total of 5 days, but this was a 9-day travel. 4 days of which, were spent in Metro Manila.

Prior to this travel, we already have itineraries set - where to eat, sightseeings and activities. Fortunately we were able to do the 85% in those items.


We plan everything on TripIt so here's our 5-day itinerary in Baguio City


Day 1

7am: Joybus from Pasay Terminal to Baguio City
3pm: Checkin to Travelite Express Hotel
4pm: Attend Mass in Baguio Cathedral
530pm: SM Baguio - Stroll around, eat dinner

Day 2


7am: Breakfast at McDonalds Veniz
830am: Wright Park Horse Stable Hill
9am: The Mansion
930am: Mines View Peak
1030am: Good Shephered
1130am: Baguio Botanical Garden
1230pm: Lunch at Sizzling Plate in front of the Palladin Hotel
130pm: Back to the Hotel
* We took out 4 hours in the afternoon for Enzo to sleep, and for me to do some work.
6pm: dinner

Day 3


730am: Breakfast at Pancake House in Camp John Hay
830: Starbucks, stroll around, picnic, bell amphitheater
1030am: Igorot Stone Kingdome tour
1230pm: Lunch at Balai Inasal Baguio

530pm: Burnham Park 6pm: dinner at SM baguio

Day 4


630am: Breakfast at Good Taste restaurant
8am: Strawberry Farm tour in La Trinidad, benguet
1030am: Colors of Stobosa
1230: Lunch GrabFood
515pm: Attend Mass on Enzo's Birthday at the Cathedral

Day 5


630am: Ordered grab food for breakfast

There are some place that we fail to visit but we'll sure to visit them when we're going back to baguio.
