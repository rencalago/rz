Venice Grand Canal at McKinley Hill

What do you like going to Venice Grand Canal?


This vibrant mall is at the heart of the city, where restaurants are all around this complex. People enjoy visiting here because of its indoor boating facility - a replica of Venice, Italy.


We've visited here last year of 2023, few weeks before christmas. This place particularly packed with people between late afternoon and evening when people get together for dinner.


Its main highlight is the canal where you can enjoy touring around on a boat for a few minutes. Such a unique experience.
