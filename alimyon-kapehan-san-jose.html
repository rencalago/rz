Alimyon Kapehan San Jose

Alimyon is located about 20 minutes from the main highway. If you are visiting the Twin Lake Balinsasayao, you may want to stop awhile for some coffee or a meal. It has a nice view of the mountain, a pond feature and native filipino-style home vibes.

We saw few development made to improve the place when we visit here early this year compared to the first time we visited here in 2018. They now have a spacious parking lot although it would be quite a challenge for a not 4x4 vehicle to park because the pavement is not all cemented and the small rocks will slide you back down lols


We ordered the typical filipino breakfast meal, and so good as always. Ooh, you need to leave your shoes at the staircase which makes like a home comfy vibes.


They have this pond feature and the pond bridge connecting to the other dining spot. I think this is a good place to have a relaxing meal. You know just chill and pause to whatever is busying you. It's nice to spend a good amount of time here to just be in the moment.
