Victorias Mountainview Resort, Tanjay City

This restaurant is about 15 to 30 minutes driving distance from the town center. Going there you'll pass through narrow roads, but it's not a big of a problem because it's all cemented pavements and not many people use this route except the locals. You won't be trailing along dirt roads.


The restaurant has large parking area at the back facing where highlight scenery is. As you enter the facade you will be greeted by the cool vibes of mix modern and traditional Filipino home style. They've got wide selection of food to choose from, and some booze if you want to.


The use of traditional filipino cogon roofing has added to its welcoming atmosphere. I was expecting to have fog when we got there because I've seen photos of it on facebook but otherwise it was a good experience. The weather was good. It was sunny, and no obstruction to the awesome views. I like it there.


They have this roofless veranda where you can take photos or just breath in the freshness of nature.


Then we took off after an hour or so. Going back to the main highway felt like we traveled half the time it took us to get there. Well we're driving downhill :))


They are open daily from 7am to 8pm. So come visit!
